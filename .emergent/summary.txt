*   **Communication & Notifications:**
    *   In-app messaging between clients and stylists.
    *   Push notifications for booking requests, acceptances, and other alerts.
*   **Admin & Platform Management:**
    *   An admin dashboard to manage stylists, review qualifications, and handle finances for tax purposes.
    *   A concierge system for complaints and feedback.

    *   Standard login/password with email verification.
